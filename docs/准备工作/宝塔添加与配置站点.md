### 添加站点
点击网站，选择添加站点
![bt_CreateSite.png](../../static/img/docs/4/bt_CreateSite.png)
配置网站
![bt_ConfigSite.png](../../static/img/docs/4/bt_ConfigSite.png)

![bt_SettingSitePseudoStatic.png](../../static/img/docs/4/bt_SettingSitePseudoStatic.png)
设置伪静态
![bt_SettingPseudoStaticForWordPress.png](../../static/img/docs/4/bt_SettingPseudoStaticForWordPress.png)

![bt_SettingSiteSSL.png](../../static/img/docs/4/bt_SettingSiteSSL.png)

![bt_CreateSiteSSL.png](../../static/img/docs/4/bt_CreateSiteSSL.png)
